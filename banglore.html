Auriga is for a few

All of us deserve our little luxuries. And that is exactly what led us to design Auriga - a small community with every facility you would expect from a much larger project

The first luxury, is location. In Bangalore today, you cannot aspire for a better location than Indiranagar. And Auriga is just off the Inner Ring Road, opposite EGL.

Second in our list of luxuries, is that you’ll be part of a small, elite community. Auriga is designed for a maximum of 12 families - so you have the exclusivity you deserve, with the companionship a community offers.

Amenities

Lifts

Auriga has 2 lifts, for just 12 families. Because we believe that time is a great luxury and we do not want you inconvenienced, in the least.

GYM

Experience top-tier fitness at Auriga's Gym. With dedicated trainers and cutting-edge equipment, reach new heights regardless of your fitness level.

Vertical Gardens

Vertical gardens, across the property, will ensure that Auriga has a very distinctive look. And of course, will be a much cooler home to stay in.

Swimming Pool

Auriga has a 20’ x 12’ swimming pool. This again, with just 12 homes, is not something you have to worry about getting crowded.

Playzone for kinds

On the roof, Auriga has a specially designed playzone for kids. With extra care taken to ensure safety goes with fun.

Manned Lobby

Auriga has a manned lobby with all security features. Designed as a place where you can meet with visitors .

Key Benefits

  • All homes at Auriga come with videophones for the security of your home.

  • At Auriga, we plan to grow our own vegetables - organically.

  • Auriga will be the first home in the country to harvest and recycle grey water, naturally.
