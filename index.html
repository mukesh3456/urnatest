Rising Tempretures

CO2 and other greenhouse gases trap heat in our atmosphere so we need to build homes that cool themselves naturally.

Climate Problems

The Solution

Conscious Living

Solar Power

Renewable energy through a grid-tied system with independent metering for each home.

Natural Light

Analysis of daylight patterns from dawn to dusk to aid optimal building design.

Water Harvesting

Rainwater is harvested based on the city’s rainfall pattern and the property contours.

Vertical Gardens

Vegetable gardens use recycled grey water and compost to grow food on your premises.

Organic Vegetables

Grown by each family in vertical and terrace gardens for consumption or barter.

Wet Waste

Your garbage is converted into compost on-site and used to grow fruit and vegetables.
